What is Emotional Intelligence?

EQ EDA definition: The ability to monitor one’s own and other’s feelings and emotions, to discriminate among them and to use this information to guide one’s thinking and actions EDA 3 steps to EQ: - attention training: to have closer awareness of mind, body and emotions - self knowledge and mastery: becoming aware of how we feel in a situation, and learn to behave accordingly - creating useful mental habits: train how we respond to thoughts and situations - very similar to CBT and dealing with NATs

Why is it important?

At work, EQ is more important than IQ and is a big differentiating factor between people at a higher level in a company In tech world EQ is linked to achievement drive and standards, influence, initiative, self confidence Huge part of what makes an outstanding leader Healthy mind and thought processes linked to happiness
